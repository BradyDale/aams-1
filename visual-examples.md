## Operational Contexts

### Athletic 

![Athletic shoes](images/athletic-shoes-1.jpg)

Athletic clothing is functional and performance-oriented.

---

### Casual

![Casual example 1](images/casual-1.jpg)  
![Casual example 2](images/casual-2.jpg)

**Casual** is the default public mode for adult men.

Key elements:
- Structured jacket
- Leather shoes or minimalist sneakers
- Simple shirt
- Dark jeans or casual slacks

---

### Elevate

![Elevate example 1](images/elevate-1.jpg)  
![Elevate example 2](images/elevate-2.jpg)  
![Elevate with pocket square](images/elevate-3-with-pocket-square.jpg)

**Elevate** signals visible social effort.

Key elements:
- Jacket
- Leather dress shoes
- Button-up or refined knit
- Slacks or dark jeans
- Optional subtle color accents

---

### Authority

![Authority example 1](images/authority-1.jpg)  
![Authority example 2](images/authority-2.jpg)

**Authority** contexts signal responsibility and competence.

Key elements:
- Structured jacket or suit
- Leather shoes
- Button-up shirt
- Slacks or suit trousers
